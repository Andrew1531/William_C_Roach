About

"Dr. William C. Roach stands at the intersection of historical Reformed theology and classical philosophy."

William C. Roach (Ph.D., Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary) is President of the International Society of Christian Apologetics. He is a prolific author and lecturer focused on the doctrine of inerrancy and the philosophical foundations of Christian faith.

Currently serving as senior pastor and adjunct professor, he spends his time training the next generation of defenders of the faith. His work is primarily focused on the defense of the Bible as the infallible Word of God against modern and postmodern critiques.
