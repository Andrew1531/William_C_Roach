About

"Defending the truth of Christianity in an age of skepticism requires intellectual rigor."

William (Bill) C. Roach, Ph.D. serves as Dean of the Norman L. Geisler School of Apologetics at Veritas International University.

He is an author, editor, and lecturer specializing in philosophical theology and classical apologetics.

Throughout his academic career, Dr. Roach has remained committed to the historical doctrines of the Christian faith, providing a robust defense for the reliability of Scripture and the objective nature of theological truth in the modern world.
